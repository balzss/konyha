Lecsó

lecsó hungarian tomato

Taken from zserbo.com

Ingredients

  • 2 large onions
  • 500 g (~1 lb) ripe tomato
  • 500 g (~1 lb) Hungarian wax pepper
  • 100 g (~1/2 cup) rice
  • 200 g (~7 oz) smoked sausage (it can be omited)
  • 1 tbsp. rendered lard
  • 2 tsp. salt
  • 200 ml (~1 cup) water

Instructions

  1. Put the rice in a strainer and rinse it.
  2. Peel the tomatoes by dropping them into boiling water. Wait until their skins start to split, scoop them out from the water and let them cool before you remove the skins.
  3. Chop the tomatoes, removing the green center.
  4. Core, seed and slice the wax peppers.
  5. Dice the onions.
  6. Put the lard in a pot on medium heat.
  7. Add the onions and sauté until onions are soft and “glassy”, not brown.
  8. Add the sliced peppers, tomatoes and the rice.
  9. Pour in the water and simmer, stirring occasionally.
  10. Slice the sausage and add to the lecsó when the rice is half-cooked.
  11. Simmer until the rice is completely cooked.
  12. Serve with white bread.
